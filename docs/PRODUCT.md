# Product Definition

## 1. Product Name

**LegionHub**

## 2. Mission

> Help every Legion TD player make better decisions.

LegionHub exists to help Legion TD players make faster, better and more informed decisions before, during and after a match.

## 3. The Problem

Competitive Legion TD knowledge is fragmented.

Players currently learn through:

- Personal experience
- Replays
- Discord discussions
- Friends
- Videos and streams
- Old guides
- Community spreadsheets
- Trial and error

This creates several problems:

1. Information is difficult to find.
2. Information may be outdated or specific to another game version.
3. New players often do not know what questions to ask.
4. Intermediate players may understand the basics but struggle to identify why they stopped improving.
5. Experienced players rely heavily on memory, intuition and community knowledge.
6. There is no structured decision-support tool focused on live Legion TD situations.

## 4. Our Solution

LegionHub will organize Legion TD knowledge into a structured platform focused on two main areas:

### Knowledge

Players can quickly find information about:

- Units
- Upgrades
- Waves
- Mercenaries
- Abilities
- Attack and armor types
- Synergies
- Weaknesses
- Game versions

### Decision Support

Players can provide their current game situation to **Legion Coach** and receive an explainable recommendation.

Example:

> "I am on Wave 8, I have 420 gold, 6 workers and this roll. What is my best next move?"

The goal is not simply to return an answer.

The goal is to explain:

- What action is recommended
- Why it is recommended
- What risk the player currently has
- What alternative actions are reasonable
- Which upcoming waves may be dangerous

## 5. Target Users

### Primary Persona — Competitive Improver

Typical profile:

- Plays Battle.net Ranked 4v4
- Approximately 1800–2300 ELO
- Already understands the fundamentals
- Wants to improve decision-making
- Struggles with consistency
- Wants to understand mistakes and improve

Primary needs:

- Better roll decisions
- Better economy decisions
- Better unit synergy
- Better wave preparation
- Better send timing
- Better understanding of losses

### Secondary Persona — New or Returning Player

Typical needs:

- Understand units
- Learn common combinations
- Understand waves
- Find updated information
- Learn the current version of the game

### Future Persona — High-Level Player

Potential needs:

- Advanced statistics
- Build optimization
- Replay analysis
- Meta comparisons
- Detailed matchup data

## 6. Initial Product Focus

The first supported environment is:

- Warcraft III: Reforged
- Legion TD Team OZE
- Version 11.4b
- Battle.net Ranked 4v4

Initial modes to research and support:

- PRCC x3
- PRCC x1
- PHCC

Support for each mode must be based on verified differences between them.

## 7. Product Principles

### Accuracy First

If we do not know something, we do not invent it.

LegionHub should distinguish between:

- Verified game data
- Calculated data
- Community consensus
- Expert opinion
- Experimental recommendations

### Fast to Use

A player may be consulting LegionHub while playing.

Important information should be accessible in seconds.

### Useful Without AI

The core product must remain useful without requiring paid AI APIs.

AI may improve the experience later, but the product must not depend entirely on it.

### Explainable Recommendations

The Coach should not simply say:

> "Build Unit X."

It should explain:

> "Build Unit X because your current frontline is sufficient, but your composition lacks the damage profile needed for the upcoming wave."

### Version Aware

Legion TD changes over time.

Game data and recommendations must eventually be associated with the version they belong to.

### Community Correctable

Incorrect or outdated information should be easy to report, review and correct.

## 8. Core User Journeys

### Search Journey

Home  
→ Search for a unit, wave or mercenary  
→ Open the result  
→ Understand the information  
→ Make a better decision

### Coach Journey

Home  
→ Open Legion Coach  
→ Enter the current game state  
→ Receive a recommendation  
→ Understand why  
→ Make a decision in-game

### Learning Journey

Home  
→ Browse guides or topics  
→ Learn a concept  
→ Explore related units, waves and strategies

## 9. What LegionHub Is Not

The initial product is not intended to be:

- A social network
- A Discord replacement
- A tournament platform
- A generic Warcraft III portal
- A live game overlay
- A fully automated replay analyzer
- A perfect game simulator
- An AI chatbot that invents answers

These ideas may be reconsidered in the future, but they are not the initial focus.

## 10. Early Success Criteria

At the beginning, success is not measured by revenue.

The first signs of success are:

- Real players use the platform.
- Players return voluntarily.
- Players find answers quickly.
- Players consider the Coach useful.
- High-level players help correct information.
- The project receives useful community feedback.
- The platform solves problems that existing resources do not solve.

## 11. Long-Term Vision

LegionHub aims to become the most useful competitive knowledge and decision-support platform for the Legion TD community.

The long-term vision may include:

- Complete game database
- Legion Coach
- Combo Finder
- Build Simulator
- Wave Explorer
- Replay Analyzer
- Data-grounded AI Assistant
- Community statistics
- Multilingual support

The product will grow based on real player needs, not simply on the number of possible features.
